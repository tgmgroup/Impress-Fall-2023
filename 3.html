Fun Things We Enjoy

Read about Agnes's trip to Tokyo Big Sight and tell us about your own favorite anime or games!

×

 I didn’t want to say yes when my son asked for my permission to attend the Toho Project Festival at Tokyo Big Sight last spring. Why? Because Tokyo is far! Anyway, since he is a big fan, I agreed with the condition that he would take me with him and, ahem, buy me lunch.

×

 Fast forward to that day, we arrived at the venue early and hopeful. But the joyful feeling soon diminished after seeing many long queues even in that rainy, gloomy, ugly weather. People came with their friends and/or families to have fun at the festival. In the end, my son had a great time!

×

 Japanese anime is very popular worldwide. You will find new anime series on social media and streaming services like Netflix. I grew up watching Dragon Ball Z, Slam Dunk, and One Piece. Even now that I am older, I still watch the newest ones like Oshi No Ko, Spy Family, and Baki.
